# Product Overview

## MH Towing Digital Management System

A comprehensive web-based towing company management system for MH Towing (Tongaat, KZN, South Africa).

### Core Purpose
Digital job card system that replaces paper-based workflows for towing operations, enabling drivers to create job cards on mobile devices in the field.

### Key Features
- Digital job cards with customer signatures
- GPS-integrated distance calculation using Google Maps
- Real-time route mapping with traffic layers
- Automated pricing calculator with configurable rates
- Professional quotation generation (WhatsApp, SMS, Email, Print)
- Spares inventory management
- Sales tracking
- Automatic backup system
- Admin dashboard with statistics
- Mobile-first responsive design

### Target Users
- Tow truck drivers (field operations)
- Office administrators (job management)
- Business owners (analytics and reporting)
